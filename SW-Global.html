Position: Ebonyi State Ad Hoc Coordinator, Nigerian Communication Commission (NCC) Sim Card Registration Project

2011 - 2012

Brief Description:


NCC flagged off the registration of all SIM cards in 28th March, 2011, in order to assist security agencies in resolving crimes and by extension to enhance the security of the state, and equally enable the commission to effectively implement other value-added services like Number portability among others while also facilitating the collation of relevant phone usage data in Nigeria.


RESPONSIBILITIES

  1. Ensure equipment and process flow
  2. Carry out field staff scheduling
  3. Manage team discipline and performance tracking
  4. Communication: Reporting, Resource requisition, Logistics
  5. Carry out security consideration briefings
  6. Oversee data capture process
  7. Oversee data logistic process
  8. Oversee registration support process
